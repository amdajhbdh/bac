## Definition

Genetics is the study of heredity and variation in living organisms.

## Key Concepts

### ADN
- **Structure:** Double hélice
- **Bases:** A-T, G-C
- **Function:** Gènes → Protéines

### Cell Division
**Mitose:** 2n → 2n (growth)
**Méiose:** 2n → n (gamètes)

### Genetic Variation
1. **Crossing-over** (prophase I)
2. **Random assortment** (métaphase I)

### Mendel's Laws
- **Uniformity F1:** All offspring show dominant trait
- **Segregation F2:** 3:1 ratio
- **Independent assortment:** Genes sort independently

## Examples

### Example 1: Monohybrid Cross
**Problem:** Cross tall (Tt) with short (tt) plants
**Solution:** 
- Parent 1: Tt (tall)
- Parent 2: tt (short)
- Offspring: 50% Tt (tall), 50% tt (short)
**Key Concept:** [[Mendel's Laws]]

### Example 2: Dihybrid Cross
**Problem:** Cross yellow round (YYRR) with green wrinkled (yyrr)
**Solution:** 
- F1: All yellow round (YyRr)
- F2: 9:3:3:1 ratio
**Key Concept:** [[Independent Assortment]]
